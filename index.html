Our Story

In 1979 there were only three Italian Restaurants in Fort Myers; one of those being LaMotta’s Italian Restaurant & Pizzeria. We have been in business for 33 years now.
Throughout these many years we have witnessed many changes in the area as far as restaurants, people, and stores that have come and gone. In our shopping center alone, the view from our front windows has changed from a Publix to now Big Lot’s.
